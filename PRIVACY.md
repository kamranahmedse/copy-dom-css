# Privacy Policy for Copy DOM + CSS

**Last updated:** March 17, 2026

## Data Collection

Copy DOM + CSS does not collect, store, or transmit any data. All processing happens locally in your browser.

## How It Works

When you activate the extension and click an element, the extension reads the DOM and computed styles of that element from the current page. The resulting HTML is placed on your clipboard. No data leaves your browser at any point.

## Permissions

- **activeTab**: Used to access the current page when you activate the picker. Only runs when you explicitly click the extension.
- **scripting**: Used to inject the picker script into the active tab.

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not communicate with any external servers, APIs, or third-party services.
